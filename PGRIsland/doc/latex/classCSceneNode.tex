\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
General scene node/object to be drawn to the window

handles size, position, direction, interaction reaction of the object, object\textquotesingle{}s geometry is loaded through A\+S\+S\+I\+MP loader or through P\+GR\textquotesingle{}s Blender exporter files
